My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

Avatar

The original Avatar was so successful because of the novelty of hyperrealism in the late 2000s, which was just developing as an art form.

Avengers: Endgame

The film has a sense of grandeur that most other Marvel films try to replicate but fail at it almost every time.

Titanic

The performances, cinematography, and still impressive effects are hard not to love.
